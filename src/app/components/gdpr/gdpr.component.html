Privacy Policy for DecoBubbles

Divers Alert Network’s Rater Community allows you to review and rate videos. Participation in the Divers Alert Network’s Rater Community is voluntary, however, if you do not provide the requested personal information, you will be unable to participate.

Divers Alert Network is the controller of your personal information and processes your personal information in connection with its Rater Community. When you join Divers Alert Network’s Rater Community we will collect your email address in order to register you and permit you to view and rate videos. The legal basis for this processing is performance of our contract with you. We do not share this personal information with any third-party. Only Dr. Papadopoulou’s research team in the Joint Department of Biomedical Engineering at UNC Chapel Hill and NC State University in collaboration with Divers Alert Network may have access to your email address. Divers Alert Network will retain your personal information until we no longer need you to rate post-dive ultrasound videos in order for us to train or validate an AI to locate bubbles.

Divers Alert Network is located in the United States. As such, your personal information will be stored and processed on servers located in the United States. The laws in the United States may not be as protective as those in your location. For example, government entities in the United States may have certain rights to access your personal information.

You have the right to access your personal information, to rectify incorrect personal information, to have your personal information erased in certain circumstances, and to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. Please note that in the event that we erase your personal information any anonymous rating data that we have collected will not be deleted. To exercise your rights, or for questions or inquiries, please contact Divers Alert Network at contact@decobubbles.com
